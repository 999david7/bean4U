Espresso Con Panna

Ingredients

  • 1 shot espresso (30 ml)
  • 2 tbsp lightly whipped heavy cream
  • Optional: cocoa powder for dusting

Preparation

  1. Brew a fresh shot of espresso into a small demitasse.
  2. Top the espresso with a dollop of lightly whipped cream.
  3. Dust with cocoa if desired and serve immediately.
