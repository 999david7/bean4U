Kopi Joss

Ingredients

  • Freshly brewed coffee
  • One small piece of hot charcoal (handled safely)
  • Sugar to taste

Preparation

  1. Brew coffee and pour into glass.
  2. Carefully insert the glowing-hot charcoal into the coffee (local tradition) — ensure safety.
  3. Stir and serve; charcoal imparts a distinct smoky flavor.
  4. Safety note: follow safe handling if attempting at home.
