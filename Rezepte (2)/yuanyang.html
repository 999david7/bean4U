Yuanyang (Coffee & Tea)

Ingredients

  • Strong black tea (1/2 cup)
  • Strong coffee (1/2 cup)
  • Sweetened condensed milk or regular milk and sugar to taste

Preparation

  1. Brew strong tea and strong coffee separately.
  2. Mix equal parts tea and coffee, sweeten with condensed milk or sugar and milk to taste.
  3. Serve hot or over ice.
