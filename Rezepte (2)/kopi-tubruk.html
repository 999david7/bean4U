Kopi Tubruk

Ingredients

  • Boiling water
  • Coarsely ground coffee (strong)
  • Sugar to taste

Preparation

  1. Place coffee grounds in a cup, pour boiling water and let steep for a few minutes.
  2. Add sugar and let grounds settle before drinking slowly.
  3. Traditionally unfiltered—sip carefully.
