Bulletproof Coffee

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brewed hot coffee
  • 1–2 tbsp unsalted grass-fed butter or ghee
  • 1 tbsp MCT oil or coconut oil
  • Optional: pinch of cinnamon or vanilla

Preparation

  1. Combine hot coffee, butter, and MCT/coconut oil in a blender.
  2. Blend 20–30 seconds until frothy and emulsified.
  3. Pour into a mug and serve warm.
