Kopi Luwak

Ingredients

  • High-quality kopi luwak beans (ethical & certified recommended)
  • Filtered water

Preparation

  1. Grind kopi luwak beans to espresso or filter grind depending on brew method.
  2. Brew as espresso or pour-over, enjoy small amounts to appreciate the flavor.
  3. Note: buy ethically sourced beans; avoid products linked to animal cruelty.
