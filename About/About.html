About bean4U

Who Am I?

Hi, I’m David Winkler – the sole cup connoisseur behind bean4U. With a lifelong curiosity for the world’s most exquisite beans and a background in both hospitality and programming, I’ve built a small but passionate service dedicated to helping coffee lovers discover the exact brew that speaks to them.

What I Do

Each day I:

  • Investigate origin stories, roast profiles, and flavor notes across a wide spectrum of coffees.
  • Create interactive, filterable lists so you can explore coffees based on aroma, body, acidity, and more.
  • Write concise “coffee-cards” that explain what you’ll taste, how best to brew, and pairing tips.
“Coffee isn’t just a drink – it’s a passport. I’m here to help you travel it.” David Winkler

Why This Matters

The market is flooded with choices, yet many newcomers feel overwhelmed or stuck ordering the same generic latte. My aim is to demystify the process:

  1. Personalisation – we recommend coffees that match your taste preferences.
  2. Transparency – every bean’s journey is traceable, from farm to cup.
  3. Education – learn how roast levels and extraction methods change flavor.

Get In Touch

Whether you’re a barista looking to deepen your menu, a coffee shop owner, or a curious home brewer — I love connecting. Drop me a line and let’s brew something great together.
